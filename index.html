About

BLASTIIC

BLASTIIC is a music producer and DJ creating modern electronic music inspired by club energy, melody and contemporary sound design.

His style combines emotional melodies with powerful, precisely crafted production designed to translate both in clubs and on festival stages.

At the core of the project is a strong focus on production quality, sound selection and the continuous evolution of a unique artistic direction.

BLASTIIC builds a world driven by atmosphere, energy and the modern aesthetic of EDM.
