About BLASTIIC

Electronic music producer shaping dark, cinematic club energy.

BLASTIIC is an electronic music producer focused on dark electronic music, cinematic tension, deep piano harmonies, and precise club foundations. The sound moves between atmosphere and impact, built for emotional pull and late-night energy.

Focus

Dark electronic music with cinematic tension and club weight.

Signature

Deep piano harmonies, atmospheric layers, and precise low-end control.

Direction

Music built for releases, future singles, and a growing artist identity across platforms.

Core Profile

BLASTIIC has been shaping sound for over twelve years, developing a style rooted in atmosphere, tension, and movement. The project sits between dark electronic music, house grooves, and dance pop energy, with a focus on musical identity rather than trend-chasing.

Current official channels include Spotify, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and SoundCloud, with the main artist website at blastiic.com.
